About Us

Jagan Institute of Management Studies is a business school in Rohini, Delhi at North India. It imparts professional education both at undergraduate level and postgraduate level in the fields of management and technology and is affiliated to Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University. It was established in 1993.
